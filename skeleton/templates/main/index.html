Welcome to the Open Humans App Demo/Dev site

This project is intended for testing, development, and demonstration of the reusable Django app for working with Open Humans, django-open-humans.

Configure this app by copying the env.sample file to .env and setting these as needed. You will need to create a project in Open Humans to get a "Client ID" and "Client secret" for your own project, and put these in your local .env file.

The code for this site can be found on Github at: https://github.com/OpenHumans/oh-app-demo. You're welcome to use it as a template or reference for how a Django-based website can connect and interact with Open Humans member accounts.

How it works (once configured)

  1. Log in to your Open Humans account
  2. Authorize your version of this project on Open Humans
    This authorizes this app to deposit your archive into your Open Humans account.
  3. Upload your data.
    You will be redirected back to this site and can then upload your data. You'll be able to access & manage your data on Open Humans.
